About Rigo Designs

Precision in Design,
Excellence in Execution

Rigo Designs Inc. is a Burbank-based building design practice led by Rigo Garcia with over three decades of experience in residential, education, commercial, aviation, and civic projects across Southern California.

From preliminary design through end of construction, we bring a hands-on, detail-oriented approach to every project. Our work is grounded in a deep understanding of building codes, DSA compliance, and the coordination of complex multi-discipline teams — ensuring every project and home is delivered with precision and care.
